Dr. BOT

Developing a Chatbot for Multilingual Healthcare Environments

About the Project

Dr. BOT is an AI-powered chatbot designed to bridge the gap between individuals and healthcare information. Using advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Bio-BERT, it enables users to upload medical reports, analyze symptoms, and receive accurate health-related responses in multiple languages.

Key Features

  • Medical report summarization
  • Multilingual support for better accessibility
  • AI-powered symptom analysis
  • Voice-based interaction for non-readers
  • Secure and privacy-compliant system

How It Works

  1. Users upload medical reports (PDF format)
  2. The chatbot extracts and summarizes critical medical information
  3. Users interact via text or voice to ask health-related questions
  4. AI-driven responses ensure accuracy and clarity
  5. Support for multiple languages enhances usability

Why Dr. BOT?

Millions of people struggle with understanding complex medical terminology, especially in multilingual environments. Dr. BOT ensures that everyone, regardless of language or literacy barriers, has access to essential healthcare information in a simple and understandable way.
